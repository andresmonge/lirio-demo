A beatiful contest

Lirio Lodge is a charming lodge located in Barra de Pacuare, the most virgin sector of the Tortuguero canals, in the Caribbean of Costa Rica. It is located directly in front of the Madre de Di-os Lagoon, an extensive and unspoiled natural canal, surrounded by tropical rainforest, and separated from the sea by only a narrow peninsula of virgin and intact tropical jungle, the famous Pacuare Reserve.

Wellness nature experience

Surrounded by three Sea Turtle conservation projects, a 2400 hectares of Biological Reserve and a labyrinth of rivers and swamps, Lirio Lodge offers different packages with boat transport, guided excursion, contact with local people and labour of the wildlife protection organizations, and a special and unique tropical architecture. Our open-air dining pavilion and bungalows will let you watch the local wildlife and listen to the sounds only found in a tropical paradise

Enjoy and relax

People can also enjoy kayaking along the lagoon border, or go for some fishing with the locals. We have two or three days packages that optionally include transportation from San Jose or you can also arrive in your vehicle to our private dock, from where you will be transfered by boat to Lirio Lodge. You will enjoy a boat tour, a visit to the Pacuare beach and small village, and a guided visit to the Sea Turtle conservation projects. The next day, a guided rainforest canal boat tour is also included, during which you can encounter exactly the same fauna and flora as you do in Tortuguero National Park.

Sea Turtle Tour

Departure everyday from 7:45 pm from the Lirio Lodge pier. It consists of a guided walk along the beach, where the guide will explain the importance of the protection and conservation of these magnificent species that are endangered.

  • Transportation included
  • Guided tour
  • Drinks included

Canals Night Tour

Come discover the wonderful nocturnal fauna of these canals, where you will find different species of animals unique to this region. Many animals will only appear at night, this tour will give you the chance of look at them.
